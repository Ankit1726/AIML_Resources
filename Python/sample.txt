1. Why AI / ML is such a big deal right now
Artificial Intelligence is no longer just a research topic. It is running search engines, recommendation systems, fraud detection, self driving, medical diagnosis, chatbots, and a lot more.

Key points:
- The global AI market is projected to grow massively from 2023 to 2033.
- AI skills are now needed across industries, not just tech giants.

- AI Engineer

3. Job market trends
Reports consistently show:
- AI is among the fastest growing job categories.
- India is seeing rapid growth in AI / ML hiring.
- Companies are hiring aggressively for AI roles.

4. Will AI kill jobs?
AI will replace some tasks but create new jobs focused on AI integration, automation, and model management.

5. Entry level difficulty
Entry level AI jobs are competitive but still achievable with the right skills and strong projects.

6. Is it worth entering AI / ML in 2026?
Yes, if you put in focused effort.
No, if you expect quick results without skills.
